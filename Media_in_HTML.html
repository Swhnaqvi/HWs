Welcome to the world of pizzas!

Pizza is a fascinating dish that was invented in Italy in betweenthe 16th and mid-18th century. The term 'pizza' was first recorded in the year 997 AD, in a Latin manuscript from the southern Italian town of Gaeta, in Lazio, on the border with Campania. Raffaele Esposito is often credited for creating the modern pizza in Naples.
